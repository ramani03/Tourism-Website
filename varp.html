Varanasi Enroute(Duration :3 Days)

⦿ Assuming your arrival time at Varanasi is by 8 a.m.

► DAY 1 :


Arrival, check-in. Boat ride on Ganga. Afternoon at 5 p.m. get your seat at Aarti Ghat, beside Daswasamedh Ghat. Enjoy Ganga Aarti till 8 p.m.


► DAY 2 :


Early morning (6 a.m.) offer puja to Viswanath Temple, back to hotel have your breakfast and set-out for s/s. Start with BHU, Sankatmochan temple, Sarnath. Back to Varanasi by 5 p.m. Shopping in Viswanath Galli.


► DAY 3 :


Exploring the famous Buddhist Shrines in Sarnath.It is a place located 10 kilometres north-east of Varanasi near the confluence of the Ganges and the Varuna rivers in Uttar Pradesh, India. The deer park in Sarnath is where Gautama Buddha first taught the Dhamma, and where the Buddhist Sangha came into existence through the enlightenment of Kondanna.
