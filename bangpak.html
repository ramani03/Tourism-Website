Banaglore Enroute(Duration :2 Days)



► DAY 1:

➊ Visit Tipu Sultan’s Summer Palace: the city’s rich past – Tipu Sultan’s Summer Palace. This 18th-century fort-cum-palace is a fine example of Indo-Islamic architecture

➋ Explore museums and historical places in Central Bangalore: The area is dotted with many historical edifices, including Vidhan Soudha, the seat of Karnataka State Legislature.

➌ Explore MG Road, Brigade Road, and Church Street: The MG Road-Brigade Road-Church Street circuit is a must-visit while in Bangalore. Make your way through these boulevards, soaking in the sights and sounds and enjoying some street food.




► DAY 2:

➊ Explore the botanical wonders at Lal Bagh Botanical Gardens: your second day in Bangalore by exploring an urban green space spread over 240 acres of land – Lalbagh Botanical Gardens. You can either walk through the garden or take a quick tour in a battery-operated vehicle.

➋ Soak in the royal charm of Bangalore Palace: Spend some time exploring the interiors of the palace including the Darbar Hall and Ballroom, and also the sprawling grounds surrounding the palace.

➌ Shop and eat to your heart’s content at Commercial Street :Apparels, handicrafts, jewelry, footwear, bags, books, or antique pieces, you will find it all in the lanes and by-lanes here. Don’t forget to take a break occasionally and have a fill at the eateries and restaurants dotting the street.
