Manali Enroute(Duration :4 Days)



Day 1: Shimla Local Sightseeing


You will visit Kufri in the first Half of the day and then Jakhoo Temple which is an ancient temple in Shimla, dedicated to the Hindu deity Lord Hanuman. It is situated on Jakhu Hill , State Museum, Mall Road, Ridge, etc.



Day 2: Shimla to Narkanda & Hatu Peak


Shimla – Narkanda – Hatu Peak – Narkanda – Shimla (a bit long day) or you can do Shimla – Kufri – Chail – Shimla (Little less hectic :)). You can of course club it with Kufri as well. However, spending time around Narkanda – Thanedar – Hatu Peak will be worth the time. Also, if you plan to go Manali over Jalori Pass, then it makes more sense to stay overnight at Narkanda.




Day 3: Manali Local Sightseeing


Manali Local sightseeing which includes Hadimba Temple, Manu Temple, Mall Road, Local Monastery, Vashisth Hot Water Springs etc. We will plan to spend some time by the riverside as well or take some trekking trails in the woods near Old Manali.




Day 4: Manali to Jagatsukh to Naggar Castle


On this day you can travel from Manali to Jagatsukh and then further to Naggar Castle. Jagatsukh is one of the offbeat places in Himachal Pradesh whereas Naggar Castle is one of the romantic places in Himachal. You should stay at Naggar and enjoy the place. It is very romantic with great views and food. Visit Roerich Museum, Art Gallery and do not miss the hike to ancient Krishna Temple at the top.
